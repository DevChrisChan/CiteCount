Introducing the all new CiteCount

Completely rewritten and introducing our new domain

More powerful and insightful, CiteCount has been completely rewritten to offer the best text counting solution for students and researchers.

Our new domain from cite.js.org into citecount.com reflects our evolution into a comprehensive academic tools platform. The .com domain better represents our expanded suite of IB academic applications, while we continue to maintain our core values of processing your text exclusively on-device for privacy and security.

Introducing more IB applications in CiteCount

With our primary audience being IB students, we have built another new tool on top of the IB exams countdown service - the IB Mock Exam Simulator. It simulates all aspects of an actual exam, from the invigilator's instructions, to reading time, to the actual timer, there is voice guidance and on screen instruction along the way, exactly like an exam. This allows you to better simulate an exam environment.
