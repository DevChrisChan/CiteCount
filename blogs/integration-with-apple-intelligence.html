Integrating CiteCount with Apple Intelligence

With the official launch of iOS 18.2 and macOS 15.2, Apple Intelligence has been rolled out to devices with new chips. CiteCount is integrated with Apple Intelligence, and this blog will introduce its usage.

Triggering the Writing Tools panel

To begin with, select any text, right click, and click Writing Tools.

Navigating the Writing Tools panel

Once triggering the panel, you will observe different tools. Experiment with each feature within CiteCount!

Generate content with Apple Intelligence & ChatGPT

Your device will ask for you to allow access to ChatGPT.

Once allowed, click Compose to generate with ChatGPT.

Let us know how you use Apple Intelligence with CiteCount!
