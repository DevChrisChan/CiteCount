Introducing IB Exam Countdowns

We have just launched a handy tool to visualize the time left until your IB Exams!

Selecting your examination countdown

We have manually created the list of IB examinations from the official examination timetables. Start viewing your countdown by selecting or searching your examination.

Sharing the examination countdown

Once selecting an examination, you can share the link by clicking Share Countdown. The Exam ID in the URL denotes your selected examination, and the corresponding examination will be automatically selected when visiting the site.
