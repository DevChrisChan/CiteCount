Downloading CiteCount

A guide to download CiteCount on your device.

CiteCount is available as a web application on citecount.netlify.app. Alternatively, it could be downloaded on your device for offline use. Follow the guide below to download CiteCount with your prefered browser. If your browser is not listed, please search for information on how to download a web application for your browser.

Google Chrome

To install CiteCount on your device through Google Chrome, click the hamburger menu (3 dots) next to the search bar, click Cast, Save, and Share, then click Install Page as App.

Safari

To install CiteCount on your device through Safari, click the share button next to the search bar, then click Add to Dock.

Confirm downloading CiteCount by clicking Add.

Firefox

Unfortunately, Firefox has discontinued the support of downloading web applications. Please download CiteCount via another browser.

Mobile Safari

To install CiteCount on your device through Safari on your iPhone, click the share button, then click Add to Home Screen.

Confirm downloading CiteCount by clicking Add.
