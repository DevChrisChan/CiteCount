Welcome to CiteCount!

CiteCount is a powerful, privacy-focused word counting tool designed specifically for students working on academic assignments. Whether you're writing an IB Extended Essay, a university paper, or any academic document, CiteCount helps you manage word counts accurately while excluding citations.

üí° Why CiteCount?

Unlike traditional word processors, CiteCount automatically detects and excludes in-text citations from your word count, following IB and academic standards. Plus, everything runs locally in your browser‚Äîyour documents never leave your device!

Quick Start Guide

  1. Open CiteCount: Navigate to citecount.com in your web browser (works on desktop, tablet, and mobile).
  2. Add Your Text: Choose one of three ways:
    • Type directly in the editor
    • Paste from your clipboard (Cmd/Ctrl + V)
    • Drag & drop a .docx or .pdf file
  3. View Your Counts: CiteCount automatically analyzes your text and displays:
    • Words without Citations (the key metric for IB)
    • Total Words
    • Characters without Citations
    • Total Characters
    • Number of Citations detected
  4. Manage Citations: Click on the Citations panel to:
    • See all detected citations
    • Click a citation to jump to it in your text
    • Toggle citations to include/exclude from count
    • View citation occurrences and word counts
  5. Save Your Work: Enable AutoSave in Settings (‚åò,) to automatically save your text as you type. Your work is saved locally in your browser.

Importing Documents

Supported File Types

  • Microsoft Word (.docx): Full support with text extraction
  • PDF (.pdf): Text extraction (images and equations removed)

Three Ways to Import

1. Drag and Drop (Easiest)

  • Simply drag your file from your computer
  • Drop it anywhere on the CiteCount editor
  • Text is automatically extracted and analyzed
  • Works with multiple files at once

2. Choose File Button

  • Click "Choose File" on the welcome screen
  • Browse and select your document
  • Upload multiple files by selecting them together

3. Paste from Clipboard

  • Copy text from any source (Word, Google Docs, PDFs)
  • Click "Paste Text" or press Cmd/Ctrl + V
  • Text is instantly analyzed
‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notes:
  • Images, equations, and special formatting are removed during import
  • Only plain text content is extracted
  • Your files are processed locally‚Äînothing is uploaded to servers
  • For best results, use .docx files

Common First-Time Questions

Do I need to create an account?

No! CiteCount works entirely in your browser without any account or sign-up. Just open the website and start using it.

Where is my text saved?

Your text is saved locally in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • ‚úÖ It's private and secure on your device
  • ‚úÖ No server uploads or cloud storage
  • ‚úÖ Persists when you close and reopen the browser
  • ‚ùå Not accessible from other devices/browsers
  • ‚ùå Can be lost if you clear browser data

Can I use CiteCount offline?

Partial support: Once you've loaded CiteCount, the app can work offline for basic text editing and word counting. However, features like file import may have limitations offline.

Is there a mobile app?

CiteCount is a web application that works great on mobile browsers. There's no separate app to download‚Äîjust visit citecount.com on your phone or tablet!
