What is CiteCount?

CiteCount is a privacy-first, browser-based word counter built for students, researchers, and anyone who writes academic work. It accurately counts words and characters while automatically excluding in-text citations, references, and bracketed content, giving you the true word count required for assignments like university assignments, IB Internal Assessments, Extended Essays, and reports. Everything runs locally in your browser, so your documents are never uploaded to a server. In addition to precise counting, CiteCount includes tools for importing Word and PDF files, highlighting detected citations, generating formatted references, and tracking reading, speaking, and handwriting time, all designed to help you meet academic requirements with confidence.
