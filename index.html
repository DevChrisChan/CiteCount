What is CiteCount?

CiteCount is a tool to count words without citations from your text. Do you have an assignment that requires you to include a word count without citations? Supporting MLA, APA, and Chicago styles, CiteCount is the tool for you.

With CiteCount, you can track and count the number of citations you have used, words you have used without citations, and characters you have used without citations. We provide advanced algorithms to ensure you get the most accurate citation countsâ€”all done within your browser.

AutoSave is integrated into the text editor of CiteCount, making sure you won't lose any changes while editing, even if you leave the site and come back later. Word counts are updated in real-time, so you can see the results as you type.

CiteCount supports languages from all over the world and strives to provide 100% accurate word counting tools. We are proud to be more accurate than word-processing software such as Microsoft Word and Google Docs.

Our features expand as users utilize CiteCount. Feel free to submit feedback here.

Most importantly, this web application is ad-free and open-source. You can use it for free, without any restrictions.
