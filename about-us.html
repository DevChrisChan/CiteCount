Our Story

CiteCount was born from a simple frustration experienced by countless students: existing word counters weren't accurate for academic work. Traditional tools counted everything, including citations and references, leading to inaccurate word counts that could affect grades and compliance with assignment requirements.

As IB students ourselves, we understood the critical importance of precise word counts. The International Baccalaureate programme has strict word limits for Extended Essays, Internal Assessments, and other coursework. A few words over the limit could mean the difference between acceptance and rejection of your work.

What started as a personal tool quickly grew into something much larger. We realized that our solution could help millions of students worldwide who faced the same challenges. Today, CiteCount serves over 100,000 students across 50+ countries, processing millions of words while maintaining our commitment to privacy and accuracy.

Why We're Different

Built by Students, for Students

Unlike generic word processors or corporate tools, CiteCount was created by people who have lived through the academic challenges our users face. We understand the stress of word limits, the frustration of inaccurate counts, and the importance of privacy in academic work.

Privacy by Design

From day one, we've built CiteCount with privacy as a fundamental principle, not an afterthought. Your documents are processed entirely on your device, ensuring that your academic work, ideas, and research remain completely private.

Academic Focus

While other tools try to be everything to everyone, we focus specifically on academic needs. This specialization allows us to provide features and accuracy that generic tools simply can't match.

Community-Driven Development

Every major feature in CiteCount has been suggested, tested, or refined by our user community. We listen to feedback and continuously evolve based on real user needs and experiences.
