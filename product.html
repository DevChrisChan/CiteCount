Welcome to CiteCount! CiteCount is a tool designed to help you count words in your text while excluding in-text citations.

CiteCount automatically detects your in-text citation and highlights it (CiteCount, 2026).

By default, all citations are not included in the Total Words count. They are visualized by a red highlight in the text. If CiteCount misrecognized an in-text citation (since CiteCount detects citations in parenthesis, like this one), you can always toggle it to count into the total word count.

You can click on a citation and CiteCount will jump to its position ("What's new in CiteCount v2", 2026).
