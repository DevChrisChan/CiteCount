Help

Exam Countdown Search or select an exam from the dropdown. The countdown shows time left until the selected exam. Click the countdown to toggle between months+days and total days view. The page title updates to match your selected exam.
Personal Dashboard Click "Create Personal Countdown" to build a dashboard of your chosen exams. You can select multiple exams, save your selection, and share your dashboard link with others. Your selection is saved in your browser for next time.
Revision Tools & Resources Access IB Exam Timer and IB Resources from the Revision Tools section for extra practice and study materials.
Settings
  • Date Format: Choose between DD/MM/YYYY and MM/DD/YYYY for all date displays.
  • Auto-scroll to Personal Dashboard: When enabled (default), the page will automatically scroll to your dashboard if you have selected exams, even if you return later.
All settings are saved in your browser and can be changed anytime.
Shortcuts & Tips
  • Press Escape to close any open modal (Help, Settings, Personal Countdown, Schedule).
  • Click outside a modal to close it.
  • Use the Share buttons to copy direct links to exams or your dashboard.
Troubleshooting If your dashboard or settings are not saving, check your browser's privacy settings or try refreshing the page. For feedback or bug reports, use the contact link in the site footer.
